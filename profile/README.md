# The Church of Nix

> **Curiosity and openness. No cults.**

Wonder is one of the oldest human impulses, and somewhere between cathedrals and laboratories it got divided up — awe handed to religion, evidence handed to science, as if you had to choose. This is a community that declines the choice: a place to take the deepest questions seriously without taking on the dogma that usually comes attached. Reverence for the mystery, rigor about the answers, and a standing refusal to turn either one into a cult.

The shared curiosity points at the nature of reality itself — the idea that the world may be not neatly quantized and finished but infinitely divisible, emergent, and finally indeterminate, with more underneath every layer no matter how far down you look. That isn't offered as a creed to accept. It's offered as an invitation: to wonder, to argue, to investigate in the open, and to hold every conclusion loosely enough that the next question can move it.

Open inquiry, good company, and a comfort with not yet knowing.
